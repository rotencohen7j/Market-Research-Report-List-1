Chlorine meters determine residual chlorine in water, and ORP meters measure oxidation-reduction potential. Dissolved oxygen meters indicate oxygen levels for aquatic life health, turbidity meters assess water clarity, and salinity meters measure salt concentration. Additional meters cater to various other specific water quality parameters.</p></p>
