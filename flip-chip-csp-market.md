<p><p>Flip Chip Chip Scale Package (CSP) is a semiconductor packaging technology that allows for higher performance and miniaturization by directly connecting the chip die to the substrate using solder bumps. Furthermore, the growing trend of Internet of Things (IoT) devices enhances the need for reliable and efficient packaging solutions. The industry is also witnessing innovations in materials and processes, such as the use of advanced dielectric materials and improved bumping technologies, contributing to enhanced performance and reliability.
