<p><p>The amphibious landing craft market comprises various types designed for specific operational roles. The LCAC (Landing Craft Air Cushion) utilizes air-cushion technology for swift, high-speed transport over water and land, making it ideal for quick troop deployments. The LCM (Landing Craft Mechanized) is a conventional landing craft designed to carry vehicles and cargo, focusing on heavier loads and beach landings. The LCU (Landing Craft Utility) provides versatile support, transporting personnel and equipment between ships and shore, suited for sustained operations.</p></p>
