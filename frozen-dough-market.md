<p><p>The frozen dough market consists of several types catering to various baking needs. Pre-fermented frozen dough involves a fermentation stage before freezing, enhancing flavor and texture. Pre-baked frozen dough is partially baked and can be further baked for convenience. Unfermented frozen dough is raw dough that requires complete preparation after thawing. Fully-baked frozen dough is entirely baked and only needs reheating, offering quick meal solutions. Each type serves different market demands, from homemade to commercial baking needs.</p></p>
