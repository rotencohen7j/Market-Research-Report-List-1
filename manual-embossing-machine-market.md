<p><p>The manual embossing machine market is primarily divided into two types: one-handed and two-handed embossing machines. One-handed embossing machines are designed for ease of use, allowing operators to create raised designs or text with a single hand, making them ideal for smaller tasks or quick applications. In contrast, two-handed embossing machines require both hands to operate, providing greater leverage and precision, which is beneficial for creating professional-quality embossing on larger materials or in high-volume production settings.</p></p>
