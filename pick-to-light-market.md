<p><p>Pick to Light market types are categorized into two main systems: Manual and Auto-Guided. In the Manual Pick to Light system, operators use lights to identify items to be picked, relying on human intervention for movement and decision-making. In contrast, the Auto-Guided system employs automated vehicles or robotics to navigate the storage area, enhancing efficiency and reducing human labor. Both systems aim to streamline the picking process, minimize errors, and optimize inventory management in warehouses and distribution centers.</p></p>
