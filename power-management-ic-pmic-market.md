<p><p>Power Management IC (PMIC) market encompasses several types, including Voltage Regulators that maintain output voltage levels, Supervisory Circuits that monitor and manage system voltages, and Gate Driver ICs that control power transistors. Battery Management ICs ensure safe charging and discharging of batteries. Voltage References provide accurate voltage outputs for circuits, while LED Lighting Driver ICs regulate current for LED lighting applications. Together, these components enhance energy efficiency and reliability across various consumer electronics and industrial applications.</p></p>
