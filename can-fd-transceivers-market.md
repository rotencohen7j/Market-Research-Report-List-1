<p><strong>The CAN FD Transceivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolated</li><li>Non-isolated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CAN FD transceivers market is segmented into isolated and non-isolated types. Isolated transceivers provide electrical isolation between the CAN network and the microcontroller, enhancing protection against voltage spikes and noise, making them ideal for safety-critical applications. In contrast, non-isolated transceivers directly connect the CAN bus to the microcontroller, offering simpler designs and lower costs. Each type serves specific applications; isolated transceivers are often used in automotive and industrial settings, while non-isolated options are suitable for low-cost implementations.</p></p>
