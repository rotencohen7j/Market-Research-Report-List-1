<p><p>The household washing machines market is primarily divided into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic washing machines handle all washing cycles automatically, including water intake and spin drying, making them convenient for users. They typically offer advanced features and require minimal human intervention. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require manual intervention to fill water and switch cycles, giving users more control over the washing process. They are often more affordable but may involve more effort in operation.</p></p>
