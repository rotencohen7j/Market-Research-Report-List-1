<p><p>Supervisory switch markets for OS&Y (outside stem and yoke) valves, butterfly valves, and post indicator valves each serve specific applications in fire protection and water management systems. OS&Y valves utilize supervisory switches to monitor valve positions and ensure they remain open or closed as needed. Butterfly valves rely on switches for flow control and operational verification. Post indicator valves feature supervisory switches that signal whether the valve is locked in the open or closed position, enhancing system reliability and safety.</p></p>
